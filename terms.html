Privacy Policy

How AI Mastery collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Last updated: March 2026
Your privacy matters. This policy explains exactly what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is protected. We do not sell your data to anyone.

1. Who We Are

AI Mastery operates the website at aimastery.uk and sells digital products including the AI Mastery ebook. For any privacy-related questions, contact us at: hello@aimastery.uk

2. What Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Purchase information: When you buy through Payhip, they collect your name, email address, and payment details. We receive your name and email address so we can fulfil your order. We never see or store your card details — these are handled entirely by Payhip and their payment processors.
  • Email address: Collected at the point of purchase so we can deliver your product and send you relevant updates. We do not add you to any marketing lists without your explicit consent.
  • Website usage data: We may use basic analytics tools (such as Google Analytics or similar) that collect anonymised data about how visitors use our website, including pages visited, time spent, and general geographic location. This data cannot identify you personally.
  • Cookies: Our website uses essential cookies to function correctly. If we use analytics cookies, they will be anonymised. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data only for the following purposes:

  • To deliver your purchased digital product
  • To send you your purchase receipt and download link
  • To respond to any support or refund requests you make
  • To send important updates about the product you purchased (such as major new editions)
  • To improve our website and product based on anonymised usage data

We will never use your data for purposes beyond those listed above without contacting you and obtaining your explicit consent first.

4. Who We Share Your Data With

We share your data only with third parties that are essential to operating our service:

  • Payhip: Our payment and digital delivery platform. They process your payment and deliver the ebook. View Payhip's privacy policy at payhip.com/privacy.
  • Email service providers: If we use an email platform to send purchase confirmations, your email address is stored with that provider solely for this purpose.
  • Analytics providers: Anonymised, aggregated website usage data only — no personally identifiable information is shared.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data with any third party for marketing purposes. Ever.

5. How Long We Keep Your Data

We retain your purchase records (name and email) for up to 3 years for legitimate business purposes including purchase support. You can request deletion of your data at any time by emailing hello@aimastery.uk and we will action this within 30 days.

6. Your Rights Under UK GDPR

As a UK resident, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you at any time.
  • Right to rectification: You can ask us to correct any inaccurate data we hold about you.
  • Right to erasure: You can ask us to delete your personal data (the "right to be forgotten").
  • Right to restriction: You can ask us to stop processing your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to portability: You can ask us to provide your data in a portable format.
  • Right to object: You can object to our processing of your personal data at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@aimastery.uk. We will respond within 30 days. If you are unhappy with how we handle your data, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal data from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. All payment processing is handled by Payhip using industry-standard SSL encryption. We do not store payment card information at any point.

8. Children's Privacy

Our products and website are intended for adults aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 18. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a minor, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any significant changes will be posted on this page with an updated date at the top. Continued use of our website or products after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions, data requests, or concerns, please contact us at:
